Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 18, 2025

Welcome to Advocate Diary. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information and case data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in accordance with global standards.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and name for authentication purposes via Google Firebase.
  • Case Data: The case numbers, party names, court details, and dates you enter are stored securely to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Usage Data: We may collect analytical data on how the app is accessed and used (e.g., dark mode preferences) to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To maintain your digital diary, forward case dates automatically, and manage client details.
  • Security: To verify your identity and prevent unauthorized access to your account.
  • Communication: To send important account updates, such as password reset emails or security alerts.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is our top priority. We utilize Google Firebase (a Google Cloud service) to store and encrypt your data. This ensures enterprise-grade security, including:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Access Control: Strict authentication protocols ensure only you can access your private case data.
  • Reliability: 99.9% uptime guaranteed by Google Cloud infrastructure.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We respect your confidentiality. We do not share your case details with third parties, advertisers, or other organizations, except as required by law.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access & Edit: You can view and edit your case files at any time.
  • Delete: You can move cases to the Recycle Bin or permanently delete them.
  • Export: You can use the "Print List" feature to export your daily cause list.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: contact.advocatediary@gmail.com
