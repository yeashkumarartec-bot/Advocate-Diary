Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Advocate Dairy collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our web application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, and mobile number (via Google Login or Email Sign-up) solely for authentication purposes.
  • Case Data: Case numbers, party names, court names, dates, and notes you enter into the diary.
  • Client Data: Client names and contact details stored in the "My Clients" section.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To authenticate your identity and prevent unauthorized access.
  • To allow you to print, search, and manage your legal schedule efficiently.

3. Data Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore). We implement strict security rules to ensure that:

  • Data Isolation: Only you can access or edit your personal case diary. No other user can see your data.
  • Encryption: Data is transmitted over a secure SSL connection (HTTPS).

4. Data Retention & Deletion

Retention: Your data remains stored as long as you have an active account.

Deletion: You can delete any case or client at any time. Deleted items move to the "Recycle Bin" where they can be permanently removed by you. If you wish to delete your entire account, please contact us.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: contact.advocatediary@gmail.com
